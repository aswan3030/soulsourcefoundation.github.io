About us

Soul Source is active in promoting the empowerment and education of women and girls in Western Kenya. Active engagement in programming for Women's Empowerment programs and educational support for young women in secondary school, college and university, and nursery school in communities in the region have become the goals of Soul Source. Learn more about Soul Source Foundation.
